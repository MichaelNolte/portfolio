michael nolte

hi, i am michael. i take web development classes at udacity. on this page i showcase my projects.

i am into web development, because the www fascinates me, and i want to learn all about it. but that is not the only reason for taking udacity classes.

web development also helps me with my job. i work as e-learning specialist at sodexo services gmbh. developing online courses requires me to visually convey content, think about user interfaces, and generate reports. it confronts me with the same questions that i face when building websites.

finally, web development counterbalances my intellectual interests. i have a classical education in social sciences. that turns me into a nerd. i like literature, history, and philosophy. coding makes me enter a different mode of thinking. i am fascinated by this new body of knowledge, and i enjoy acquiring it.
